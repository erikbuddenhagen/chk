History

With courage, foresight, and determination, The Children's Home of Kingston was conceived and established by a small group of warm-hearted, energetic women in December 1876.

Leadership

PROGRAMS

The Children's Home of Kingston provides programs and services for males 10 to 21 whose circumstances preclude a less restrictive setting, and may benefit from a highly structured therapeutic environment.
